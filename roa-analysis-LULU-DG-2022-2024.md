# ROA Analysis: Lululemon vs Dollar General (2022–2024)

**Skill:** `/roa-analysis LULU DG 2022 2023 2024`

---

## Net Profit Margin % Over Time

| Year | Lululemon | Dollar General |
|------|----------:|---------------:|
| 2022 | 10.54% | 6.38% |
| 2023 | 16.11% | 4.29% |
| 2024 | 17.14% | 2.77% |

## Asset Turnover Over Time

| Year | Lululemon | Dollar General |
|------|----------:|---------------:|
| 2022 | 1.45 | 1.30 |
| 2023 | 1.36 | 1.26 |
| 2024 | 1.39 | 1.30 |

## ROA % Over Time

| Year | Lululemon | Dollar General |
|------|----------:|---------------:|
| 2022 | 15.24% | 8.31% |
| 2023 | 21.86% | 5.39% |
| 2024 | 23.87% | 3.61% |

---

## Key Takeaway

Lululemon's ROA grew from 15% → 24% over three years. Dollar General's fell from 8% → 3.6%. The entire difference comes down to margin: Lululemon kept more of each sale every year, while Dollar General's margins were squeezed by rising costs with almost no cushion to absorb them.
